Wordle Clone

Hi! This is a wordle clone written in Python. To play this game, type a 5 letter word in the box below and press 'Enter' / 'Return'. If you guess a letter right and it's at the right position, it'll turn green. If it's in the word but not at the right position, it'll turn yellow. Finally, if it's not in the word at all, it'll turn red. You have 6 chances and these 'colour-hints' to guess the right word. Currently, the game updates with a new word everyday. Enjoy!

Note: This project was inspired by the original game- 'Wordle', which is much better than this spin-off. Please have a look at that as well!
